Portable Fonts for Use By The 5L Engine
=======================================

These are various free fonts which we are allowed to ship with our programs.
These fonts come from DFSG- and OSI- compliant font packages found on
a typical Debian GNU/Linux system.

Descriptions, copyright information, and licensing can be found in the
individual subfolders.

***************************************************************************
******** If you add fonts to this directory, make an entry in the *********
******** appropriate subfolder listing the fonts and detailing    *********
******** their copyright license.                                 *********
***************************************************************************
